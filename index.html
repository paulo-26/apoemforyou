Hi.

If you're reading this, then there's still an "outside."

I don't know exactly when this will reach you,
but I know why I'm writing.

Here, time doesn't move.
It repeats.

And repeats.

And repeats.

Wally thought it would just be a bad dream.
Maybe he thinks I'm still sleeping.

But I think.

I remember.

I write.

My friends are still with me.

Or broken versions of them.

They smile when they should smile,
they speak when something is expected,
and they look at me as if I were part of the scenery.

I keep trying to keep everything organized.

I keep saying that everything will be alright.
Old habits die hard.

Sometimes I play the piano.
The keys are always in tune,
but the music never ends.

It always goes back to the beginning.

If this seems confusing, I apologize. It's not easy to explain a place that shouldn't exist.

I'm not asking to be saved.
Not yet.

Just…
if you notice something wrong,
if the silence lasts longer than it should,
if someone seems trapped in a role they didn't choose—

remember me.

As long as someone remembers,
this place won't win.

— Monika
